What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They allow the website to remember your actions and preferences over a period of time, so you don’t have to keep re-entering them whenever you come back to the site or browse from one page to another.

How We Use Cookies

Our cookies are essential for the operation of our website and cannot be opted out of. They are used to authenticate and identify returning users securely through Kinde to keep users logged in, track user sessions, and maintain security standards.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Session Cookies: These are used to maintain your session and remember your login on our site. They are essential for keeping you logged in as you navigate through our Services.
  • Security Cookies: We use these cookies to enable and support our security features, and to help us detect malicious activities and violations of our User Agreement.
